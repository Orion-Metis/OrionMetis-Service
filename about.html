About Us

  • ORION METIS was established in the year 1991 to Manufacture Fluxes and Chemicals for the Electronics Industry by Professionals well versed in Soldering Technology & Flux Chemistry. The consistent quality of products coupled with Technological expertise and prompt delivery schedules has positioned ORION METIS as an accomplished manufacturer in a competitive market over the past several years. The organisation has been catering to many reputed PCB Fabrication and Assembly companies spread all over India with Exports to neighbouring countries.

  • Accredited with ISO 9001:2000 IN THE YEAR 2001 AND LATER RECERTIFIED TO ISO 9001:2015 in the year 2016 establishes its commitment to highest quality and customer satisfaction.

  • Assembly Fluxes Manufactured to meet International Standards and proprietary formulations for PCB Fabrication find wide acceptance by many Manufacturers.

  • The choice of Chemicals employed in the formulations are carefully selected based on the knowledge & Experience gained over the years resulting in high quality products at competitive prices.

  • No Clean Fluxes for electronic assemblies and solar fluxes specially developed for solar panel assemblies using the stringer and tabber machines are products of significance. These products though very active contain low solids thereby the non-corrosive residues are minimal which needs no cleaning due their high surface insulation.

  • Many products are RoHS Compliant which are tested at external laboratories and we also give our declaration if required by the customer.

  • Particular emphasis is laid on the development of products to suit customer specific requirements through close interaction which is the highest priority of ORION METIS
