Introduction

Expertise in Flux and chemical manufacturing for electronics since 1991 catering to many reputed PCB fabrication and Assembly companies spread all over India with exports to neighbouring countries.

What we do

We manufacture Fluxes and chemicals for Electronics industry by professional well versed in soldering technology and Flux chemistry. The consistent quality of products coupled with Technological expertise and prompt delivery schedules has positioned Orion Metis as an accomplished manufacturer in a competitive market over the past several years.

Assembly Fluxes manufactured to meet international standards and propreitory formulations for PCB facbrication find wide acceptance by many manufacturers.

The choice of chemicals employed in the formulations are carefully selected based on the knowledge and experience gained over the years resulting in high quality products at competitive prices.

Certification
